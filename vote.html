Time Left to Vote For Our Autumn Meeting

Voting is different this month!

We're going to read a collection of suggested short stories this month. Each person can suggest up to two stories and the page limit per story is 50 pages. Once we've collected all our stories, they'll be compiled into a document that will be sent out to the group to read. When you suggest a story please include the author and the title. If you have any other information about the story (like a link to where it can be read or the title of the book it was published in) please include the information in your suggestion. If you don't have that information, that's ok! Feel free to suggest with just a title and author.
